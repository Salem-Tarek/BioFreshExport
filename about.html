About Us

Bio Fresh Company for Exporting Agricultural Products. The company is located in the Arab Republic of Egypt. And due to Egypt's good reputation in this field and our great experience in this field, we export to all parts of the world, especially the European Union countries, as well as all Arab countries to provide safe healthy food in terms of quality, packaging and shipping. We pledge to provide excellent products of high quality to our customers and adhere to international specifications according to European specifications. Our goal is the satisfaction of our customers.
